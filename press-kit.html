Full Bio

Tyler McCulloch is a veteran creative leader with over two decades of experience shaping interactive entertainment. As Game Director at Cloudhead Games, he directed the content strategy for Pistol Whip, the award-winning VR rhythm-shooter that has inspired 500+ community remixes.


His career spans industry-defining studios: at Rockstar Games, he contributed to the combat system for Bully—a system that influenced future titles like Sleeping Dogs and Batman: Arkham Asylum. At Archiact Interactive, he co-directed Journey to Foundation alongside Mass Effect veterans and led creative on Horizon Worlds experiences that topped Meta's retention charts.


Tyler's design philosophy centers on emotional resonance and player agency. Whether directing studio-wide creative vision or hands-on scripting prototypes, he brings both strategic thinking and technical fluency to every project. He thrives on collaboration across art, engineering, and production, and is passionate about mentoring the next generation of game designers.
